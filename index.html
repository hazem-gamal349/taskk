Beauty salon furniture & equipment

Glam Furniture is leader in the design and manufacturing of furniture and equipment for
beauty salons and spas. We constantly work with the best suppliers to engineer the most
innovative solutions for our clients. Glam Furniture salon equipment products are
manufactured in USA and sold in over 40 countries worldwide through our network of
selected dealers as well as our flagship showrooms in New York, Paris and Cologne.
